Below The Line is coming!

We're working on a brand new ballot editor and we're planning to be online in plenty of time for the 2016 Australian Federal Election.

The new Senate voting rules mean that we no longer need to demystify the group voting tickets but we'll still be providing ballot editors for both houses.

Candidates are declared on June 10th. The full site will be live after that when the ballots are released.
